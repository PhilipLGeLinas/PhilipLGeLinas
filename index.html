I studied biology and chemistry at the University of Washington in pursuit of a career in medicine or dentistry. After shadowing a variety of health-care professionals, I realized that I was more interested in biotechnology. I received a full-ride scholarship from Washington State University to pursue my Bachelor's degree in Software Engineering. I spent the summers of 2018 and 2019 interning for MuleSoft and Space Dynamics Laboratory.
I am a satellite software engineer. I lived in Tokyo, Japan for two years and learned Japanese.
I am a black belt in Shudokan karate. I am an eagle scout.
